Ace Hotel New Orleans

The fashionable rooms come with bespoke bed linens and handcrafted furnishings. All include free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and minibars, plus designer toiletries. Suites add living rooms with turntables and acoustic guitars, while an upgraded bi-level suite offers a terrace and a wet bar. Room service is offered 24/7. There are 5 chic restaurants and bars. Other amenities include a rooftop pool, a 24-hour gym and a retro photo booth. There's regular live entertainment. Pets are welcome.
